    ● Little taste of paradise, come and discover Comporta and its surroundings




    Heavenly beaches:


    ☛ Between the mouth of the Sado and Zambujeira do Mar, the Alentejo coastline rivals any other in its diversity of attractions.

    ☛ Here, long golden sandy beaches alternate with enchanting coves nestled between cliffs.

    ☛ The choice is vast: from the Troia peninsula, heading south, you'll find the wonderful beaches of Comporta, Pinheiros, Galé, Melides, Santo André, São Torpes, Morgavel, Vale Figueiros, Porto Covo, Vila Nova de Milfontes, Odemira...




    ● Just an hour from Lisbon, this wild land, now the talk of the town, is a haven for lovers of simplicity. To get to grips with this little corner of the world, set down in a fishermen's hamlet or on a seaside terrace. Bathing in nature, strolling through the rice paddies, in the middle of the dunes... you can indulge in the simple pleasures of life between sea and sky.
