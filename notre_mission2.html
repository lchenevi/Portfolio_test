    Our Mission

    Property Consultant


    ⸻⸻⸻⸻





    Role and tasks:



    ● Buying a property can be a complex operation. The buying process is fairly straightforward, but requires a good knowledge of the property market.


    ● Once you've decided on your search criteria, we'll look for the property that meets your requirements and guide you through every stage of the process.

    The process:



    ● Once we have determined your budget and your search criteria, we will show you properties that are on the traditional estate agency market as well as off-market properties that allow you to buy directly from private individuals who do not wish to work with estate agencies.

    ● We represent you and your interests, so we'll be the mediators who negotiate the best possible terms for you.

    ● We will guide you through all the stages leading up to your purchase.

    Advantages:



    ● We have many local contacts.

    ● We work for you.

    ● We have an excellent knowledge of the local players, including lawyers, architects, builders, banks and various companies.

    ● We negotiate the best price for your home.
