    Why "Ethics"?

    A concept of redistribution

    ⸻⸻⸻





    ➜Ethics is a reflection on the values that guide and motivate our actions. Because we want to make the most of the different skills we have acquired in our professional careers and thus give more meaning to our lives, we have decided to redistribute most of our profits to institutions or associations in the following 3 sectors:


    ● Environmental


    ● Social


    ● Cultural




    In order to involve you in the process of redistributing your commission, you can choose one of the lucky beneficiaries of your action from among the many partnerships we have.

    Environmental ⤵︎



    Because the environment is at the heart of our concerns, we believe it is essential to support this cause through our actions. We have partnerships with a number of local, national and international nature protection associations.

    Social ⤵︎



    Social action refers to all the means by which a society acts on itself to preserve its cohesion, in particular through actions aimed at helping the most vulnerable people or groups to live better.



    Cultural ⤵︎



    A central element of a dynamic society, culture is expressed in the way we tell our stories, celebrate, remember the past, entertain ourselves and imagine the future. Our creative expression helps us define ourselves and see the world through the eyes of others. Help us to promote the local development of cultural and artistic activities such as dance, music, theatre, film, visual arts, reading, writing, literature, etc.
